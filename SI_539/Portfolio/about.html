About Me

Hi, I'm Denver. I’m an aspiring UX designer with a background in psychology. This has given me varied experience in doing research projects with both qualitative and quantitative methodologies offering key user insights to class based clients.

I have an analytical mind, which helps me inform my design decisions and also have a greater attention to detail. I work well collaboratively as well as independently and have a great appreciation for the phrase “you are not the user”.

I’m currently looking for UX Research and Design internships that offer opportunities to work with diverse teams and projects and engage in both design and research.
